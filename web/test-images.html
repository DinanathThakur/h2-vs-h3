Multiplexing Benefits

HTTP/2 and HTTP/3 can load multiple resources simultaneously over a single connection, unlike HTTP/1.1 which requires multiple connections or sequential loading.

  • HTTP/2: Multiplexing over TCP connection
  • HTTP/3: Multiplexing over QUIC streams
  • Benefit: Reduced connection overhead
  • Result: Faster overall page load times
